Friends and Villans

Enduring Oak & Hazon Gamizon

Two adventures out to make a buck but wont hesitate to swindle people for money like the rat bastards they are.

Mercutio

Recently taking over the position as leader of Clan Tonberry, the guild of adventurers in Ashtwall, Mercutio has been hard at work cleaning up the mess left by his predecessor. Rooting out corruption, and pruning less than stellar members is always an arduous task, but one that he has excelled at thus far.

Sunni

Sunni is a kind and creative Hume woman who runs Sunni's Sweet Buns, a bakery in upper Ashtwall. Her shop is warm and cozy and the perfect place to enjoy one of her signature giant sweet buns.

Thata Ta'yuun

Thata Ta'yuun, the lavender-furred Miqo'te brawler that our heroes have run across on more than one occasion. Her motivations remain unclear, and her allegiances are questionable, but if one thing is for certain, there are none so far who can stand up to the sheer brutality and efficiency of her fighting style.
